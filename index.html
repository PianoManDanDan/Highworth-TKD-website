About

Tae Kwon-Do is a Korean Martial art, officially founded by General Choi Hong Hi in 1955. Tae Kwon-Do (literally, 'The art of Kicking and Punching') is a defensive martial art focusing on self-defense and discipline.

Highworth Tae Kwon-Do club was started in 19XX by the head instructor, Mrs Alison Lee.

Learning Tae Kwon-Do is an excellent way to improve fitness and flexibility, whilst also learning self-defense.

We offer family friendly classes for people of all ages. Our classes offer a great way to make new friends, while getting fit at the same time.

We train in the original, ITF (Chang-Hon) style of Tae Kwon-Do.

Links

Below are some links to some of our associated organisations, as well as links to find out more information about Tae Kwon-Do.


Deedigan Martial Arts

Master Deedigan Martial Arts Academies is our parent organisation, and sets out our training syllabus. Their chief instructor, Master Don Deedigan, is an 8th Degree black belt with over 30 years of experience in Tae Kwon-Do.


ITF Union

The ITF Union is a global organisation that seeks to unite many different ITF style Tae Kwon-Do organisations under one banner. We are affiliated with them through Master Deedigan.
